What we Do

History

There was a need to solve for the animal homeless crisis in our community. One guy, Cesar Millan , decided that he wanted to make a change. Our mighty founder started picking up stray dogs and cats to provide food and a place to stay.

Our mighty founder started off with a vision to help animals globally and that vision became a reality. Cesar began to realize that his small home cannot accommodate for the demand. After saving up for a deposit, he was able to purchase a large building to house all of his pets. This marks the moment when Paws N Tails was born.

Since 1987 , Paws N Tails offers pet adoptions, affordable medical services such as vaccinations and spaying/neutering services, and education programs to help drive for a healthy environment for humans and animals. We are the very first organization in our county to create standardized support programs for our animals and service disadvantaged families. Recently, we were recognized as the world's first exemplar of an animal shelter. This achievement is huge since there are only 10 in the world like us. We are trusted by the Association of Shelter Veterinarians.

Our Values

Mission

Our mission is rescue lost and abandoned animals in our local community, assist animals by providing food, shelter and vet care to those in need, promoting spay and neutering to end the population of unwanted pets, and provide adoption opportunities of animals to loving homes.

Vision

Our vision to strive for a world where every animal has the medical help it needs to sustain longevity and a forever loving-home.

How we Work



Rescue and Rehabilitation

We respond to dangerous situations in which we provide care and shelter for strays. While our location does not shelter animals beyond cats and dogs, we relocate them and reinsure they are triaged to the right location and receive the medical help they need.

Education and Training

We provide the tools that encourage our community to create a loving enviroment for their pets. We believe in investing not only in educating our critters, but our pet owners as well.

Services

We strive to provide top-tier services for our pets and pet owners coming from different backgrounds. We believe all animals deserve the same level of attention and care.
